Our Story

The Heart of East Texas Hospitality

Nestled in the heart of Palestine, Backroads Dance Hall is the place locals come to unwind, cut loose, and feel at home. We've got cold drinks, a big dance floor, live music on the weekends, and a crowd that knows how to have a good time.

Whether you're here for karaoke with friends, a pool tournament, or to two-step to live Texas country on a Saturday night — you're always welcome at Backroads. We keep it real, keep it fun, and keep the drinks cold.
